About Me

Corporate Discipline Meets
Amazon & E-Commerce Expertise

Hi! I'm Stephen Reyes, a certified Amazon Virtual Assistant with a strong foundation built across 12+ years of professional experience in procurement, document control, and e-commerce.

I started my career as a Purchase Officer (2013–2017) at Red Viper Ventures, developing sharp skills in procurement, vendor coordination, and budgeting. I then moved into Document Control at Aboitiz Construction (2018–2021), where I built deep expertise in organized reporting, process management, and meticulous documentation across large-scale construction projects.

In January 2022, I transitioned into Amazon e-commerce as a Product Analyst & Researcher at Emporium Virtual Assistant Services (EVAS), gaining 5 months of hands-on experience with product research tools and Seller Central workflows. In 2024, I directly managed an Amazon UAE Marketplace store — handling listings, account health, FBA/FBM operations, and customer messaging. Since August 2022, I've continued building operational discipline as a Purchase Officer & Document Controller at Al Turath Al Aseel Contracting LLC in Dubai — skills that directly fuel my precision and professionalism as an Amazon VA.
