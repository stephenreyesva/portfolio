About Me

Corporate Discipline Meets
Amazon & E-Commerce Expertise

Hi! I'm Stephen Reyes, a certified Amazon Virtual Assistant with a strong foundation built across 13+ years of professional experience in procurement, document control, and e-commerce.

I started my career as a Purchase Officer (2013-2017), developing sharp skills in procurement, vendor coordination, and budgeting. I then moved into Document Control (2018-2021), where I built deep expertise in organized reporting, process management, and meticulous documentation across large-scale construction projects.

Since January 2022, I've been working as an Amazon & E-Commerce Virtual Assistant, gaining hands-on experience with product research, Seller Central workflows, listing optimization, and store management. In 2025, I directly managed an Amazon UAE Marketplace store handling listings, account health, FBA/FBM operations, and customer messaging. Most recently since May 2026, I've been actively supporting a UK-based e-commerce brand as a part-time remote VA, handling product uploads, order processing, customer support, and day-to-day store operations.
